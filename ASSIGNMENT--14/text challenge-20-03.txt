Assignment Name: Text Challenges

Objective:
To analyze real-world messy text data and identify common issues such as slang, emojis, and typographical errors, and suggest appropriate preprocessing techniques.

Sample Messy Sentences:
Heyyy!!! How r u??? 😊
I'm sooo tired rn 😴
This movie is lit 🔥🔥
LOL that was funny 😂
Plzz send me the notes ASAP
I cant believe this happened!!!
OMG!!! This is amazing 😍

U should try this app 👍
Idk what to do now 😕
This is gr8 work 👏
Hahaha 😂😂 that joke!
Wat r u doing??
Thnx for helping me 😊
This product is sooo bad 😡
I luv this place ❤️
It’s not working properly 😤
Bcoz of rain, match cancelled ☔
That was awesomeeee!!!
Gonna sleep now 😴
Issues Identified:
Slang/Short Forms: r, u, plzz, lol, idk, thnx, luv, bcoz
Emojis: 😊 😂 😴 🔥 😍 😡 ❤️
Typos/Repetition: heyyy, sooo, yummyyy, awesomeeee
Punctuation Noise: !!! ???
Inconsistent Case: Mix of uppercase and lowercase
Preprocessing Techniques Required:
Convert text to lowercase
Remove punctuation and special characters
Remove or handle emojis
Correct spelling and repeated characters
Expand slang/abbreviations
Remove stopwords
Apply tokenization
Use lemmatization or stemming
Conclusion:

This assignment helped in understanding the challenges of real-world text data and the importance of preprocessing before applying machine learning models. Proper text cleaning improves the accuracy and efficiency of NLP systems.
