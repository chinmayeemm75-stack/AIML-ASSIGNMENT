AI Around Me – 10 Daily Life Applications

1.Google Maps
Uses ML to predict traffic, suggest fastest routes, and estimate arrival time based on past and real-time data.

2.YouTube
Recommends videos using ML models that learn from your watch history, likes, and viewing patterns.

3.Amazon
Suggests products using recommendation systems based on your browsing history and similar users’ behavior.

4.Gmail
Uses ML for spam detection and smart replies by analyzing email content and user behavior.

5.Google Assistant
Uses speech recognition and NLP models to understand voice commands and respond intelligently.

6.Instagram
Uses AI for face detection, filters, and personalized feed ranking based on user interaction.

7.WhatsApp
Applies ML for spam detection and auto-suggestions (like predictive text and emojis).

8.Spotify
Recommends songs using ML models trained on listening history and user preferences.

9.Google Photos
Uses computer vision ML to recognize faces, objects, and organize photos automatically.

10.Phone Pay
Uses ML for fraud detection by analyzing transaction patterns and identifying unusual activities.
